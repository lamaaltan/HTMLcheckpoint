About Oprah

Oprah Gail Winfrey was born on January 29, 1954, in Kosciusko, Mississippi, U.S. She overcame a difficult childhood and went on to become one of the most influential women in the world.

Her dedication to empowering others and her passion for storytelling has made her an iconic figure in the entertainment industry.
